Inventory & POS Management System

1. Project Overview

The Inventory & POS Management System is a web-based application designed to efficiently manage products, stock, sales (Point of Sale), and users within a business.
The system allows Admin, Manager, and Staff users to perform tasks based on their assigned roles, ensuring smooth and secure operations.


2. Project Objectives

* Manage inventory and stock in real time
* Enable fast and accurate sales through a POS system
* Control user access with role-based permissions
* Reduce manual work and human errors
* Generate business reports for better decision-making


3. User Roles & Responsibilities

i. Admin

* Full system access and control
* Create, update, and delete users (Manager, Staff)
* Manage categories, suppliers, and products
* View sales and stock reports

ii. Manager

* Manage products and inventory
* Manage suppliers
* Monitor sales activities
* Track staff performance

iii. Executive

* Perform sales using the POS system
* Generate invoices
* Limited access (no permission to manage system data)


4. Core Modules

	i. Authentication & Authorization

	* Secure login and logout system
	* Role-based access control (Admin, Manager, Staff)

	ii. Category Management

	* Add, edit, and delete categories
	* View products by category

	iii. Supplier Management

	* Add, edit, and delete suppliers
	* Manage supplier contact and address information

	iv. Product Management

	* Add products with category and supplier
	* Manage price, quantity, and status (Active / Inactive)
	* Update product stock

	v. Inventory / Stock Management

	* Real-time stock updates
	* Track stock-in and stock-out
	* Low stock alerts (optional)

	vi. POS (Point of Sale)

	* Search and select products
	* Add products to cart
	* Update quantities dynamically
	* Automatic total calculation
	* Invoice generation

	vii. Sales Management

	* Record daily and monthly sales
	* View sales history
	* View invoice details

	viii. Reports

	* Sales reports
	* Stock reports
	* User-wise activity reports


5. Technology Stack

* Backend: Laravel (PHP)
* Frontend: Blade Template, Bootstrap
* Database: MySQL
* Authentication: Laravel Authentication System


6. Key Features

* User-friendly interface
* Role-based dashboards
* Secure authentication and authorization
* Fast and reliable POS system
* Scalable and maintainable architecture
